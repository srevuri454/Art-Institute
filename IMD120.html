Course Description

IMD 120 Programming Logic course is offered at the Art Institute of San Antonio, TX.

In this course students will develop and refine basic programming skills needed to design, develop and produce practical applications with a specific scripting or programming language.

Class Hours

IMD131 Introduction to Scripting Languages meets twice per week for 11 weeks. Each session is 4 hours, divided between lecture and lab time. You will have 44 hours for the course, 22 lecture and 22 lab.

 

Assignments and Grading

You will be assessed through labs, quizzes, and project work.

20%    In-class exercises and participation
20%    Weekly Assignments and Quizzes
20%    Project 1
10%    Midterm
20%    Project 2
10%    Final Exam

Project #1: Create your Personal Webpage
In this project you will create your personal webpage. This project will provide you an opportunity to practice the basic concepts of HTML. You will not use any Webpage editors such as Dreamweaver or FrontPage for this project. You may use text editor to create this page. Personal webpage may contain information about yourself such as your education background, your favorite sports or movies, your hobbies, your interests etc.
